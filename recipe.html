Recipe for prepairing fried green bananas

Ingredients

  1. Five green bananas
  2. Two Onions
  3. Three Tomatoes
  4. Cooking oil
  5. Salt
  6. Dhania

Procedure

  1. Put the cooking oil in a sufuria and allow it to boil for 3 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped onions and add the into the boiling oil and leave it for five minutes.
  3. Now add the sliced tomatoes into the sufuria and leave it for three to four minutes and then add one spoon-full of tablesalt.
  4. When the tomatoes have been allowed to simmer for about 4 minutes, add the chopped dhania and give it 2 minutes.
  5. Add the peeled green bananas into the sufuria and aloow it to simmer for about 1 to 15 minutes.
  6. The meal is now ready to be served with other meals.
